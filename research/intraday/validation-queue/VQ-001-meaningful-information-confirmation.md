# VQ-001 — Meaningful Information Confirmation

- Candidate: [AC-004](../architecture-candidates/AC-004-confirmation-meaningful-information.md)
- Extracted principle: [EP-004](../extracted-principles/EP-004-shared-data-is-not-independent-confirmation.md)
- Sources: YT-006; YT-007
- Status: QUEUED
- Owner: Unassigned

## Validation Question

Can meaningfully independent information be distinguished from repeated transformations or descriptions of the same underlying data at an architecture level?

## Required Evidence Categories

- Information provenance and dependency research
- Independent review of confirmation semantics
- Conflicting cases where shared-source evidence still adds legitimate information

## Risks

- False acceptance: legitimate corroboration may be discarded or false confirmation preserved.
- False rejection: redundant descriptions may be treated as independent evidence.

No taxonomy, formula, weighting, or threshold is selected.
