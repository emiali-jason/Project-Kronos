# WRK-001 — Option Buying Video Learnings

**Status:** Draft Research  
**Source:** [KRONOS Options Research — Option Buying Video Learnings](https://docs.google.com/document/d/16Gw0i6Xkyf7WH_kiTe5SQS6JUxZHkNQ_ZuO632TzDm8)  
**Source status:** Accepted Phase 1 working note

## Observation

The note synthesizes three educational videos and keeps their recurring buying concepts separate from indicator settings and source claims.

## Evidence

- Direction alone is repeatedly described as insufficient.
- Movement speed, delta, theta, IV, time to expiry, liquidity, and spread recur as concerns.
- Choppy or conflicting conditions are presented as difficult.
- Predefined loss, exit, and invalidation recur as process themes.
- Fixed premiums, times, indicators, and performance claims remain unsubstantiated.

## Possible Interpretation

The note supplies a useful taxonomy for future buying research: underlying context, contract behavior, execution friction, and invalidation.

## Open Questions

- Which recurring concepts survive independent and non-promotional evidence?
- What data can distinguish a useful explanation from a repeatable relationship?
- How should buying evidence be separated from the selling research stream?

## Not Retained

The note is synthesis, not independent validation, architecture, or trade logic.
