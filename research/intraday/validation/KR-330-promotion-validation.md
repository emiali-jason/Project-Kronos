# KR-330 Promotion Validation

## Purpose

Collect evidence regarding promotions between:

```text

↓
WATCH
↓
BUY READY
↓
BUY NOW
```

This document records promotion timing, available evidence, and duration spent in each state. It does not recommend changes.

## Evidence Collection Rules

- Record only Trading Desk-reviewed observations.
- Preserve blanks when timings or states are unavailable.
- Do not infer promotions that were not visible.
- Do not evaluate trade outcome in this document.
- Do not recommend thresholds, architecture, or Pine changes.
