## Validation Question

Can expected uncertainty and adverse sequences be distinguished from behavioural non-compliance, regime mismatch, and architecture failure without hindsight?

## Required Evidence Categories

- Sequence-risk and uncertainty research
- Regime and failure-classification literature
- Independent review of decision-validity attribution

## Risks

- False acceptance: genuine failure may be normalised.
- False rejection: ordinary uncertainty may trigger false architecture-failure conclusions.

No sequence length, tolerance, formula, or threshold is selected.
