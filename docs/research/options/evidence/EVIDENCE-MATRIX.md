## Evidence Classes

- **Exchange material:** May establish current contract mechanics within its stated scope and date.
- **Practitioner book or educational video:** Generates concepts, cautions, and hypotheses; does not independently validate performance.
- **Working note:** Preserves user observations or prior synthesis; does not become more authoritative through repetition.
- **Pending:** Metadata only; no content may be inferred.
