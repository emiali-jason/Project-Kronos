# Candidate Principles

**Status:** Draft Research  
**Authority:** None

These are recurring research propositions. They are not approved platform principles, architecture, trading rules, or implementation requirements.

| ID | Candidate principle | Evidence basis | What remains unresolved |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CP-001 | Evidence should precede Options architecture. | Repository governance and the MA-Options research charter | What evidence is sufficient to authorize Phase 2? |
| CP-002 | Observation, evidence, interpretation, and decisions should remain distinguishable. | Accepted working notes and stress-governance questions | Required provenance and review method |
| CP-003 | Option risk is dynamic and position-level, while leg-level detail remains necessary. | BK-001; rolling-position observations | Required data quality and aggregation method |
| CP-004 | Premium received is compensation for risk, not income by default. | BK-001; selling playlist | Product-specific empirical validation |
| CP-005 | Theta and gamma should be studied together, particularly near expiry. | BK-001; expiry-session example | Instrument- and expiry-specific behavior |
| CP-006 | Direction alone is insufficient for short-horizon option buying. | Three buying videos; BK-001 | How movement, time, volatility, and friction interact |
| CP-007 | Liquidity and executable spread are part of risk, not post-analysis details. | MCX option-chain lesson; BK-001 | Live spread, depth, slippage, and rejection evidence |
| CP-008 | Expiry, exercise, assignment, and futures devolvement are part of the position lifecycle. | Official MCX material | Broker-specific handling and operational evidence |
| CP-009 | A rolling or adjusted position should be evaluated through its full lifecycle, not only its opening payoff. | Selling working notes | Complete order, fill, Greek, margin, and intent history |
| CP-010 | Defined-risk structures deserve separate study from naked selling. | BK-001; masterclass and expiry-session examples | Comparative stress, cost, liquidity, and capital evidence |
| CP-011 | Cross-market context must be tested rather than assumed causal. | COMEX-first / MCX-confirmation observations | Lead-lag, currency, session, and contract effects |
| CP-012 | Source-specific numbers remain claims until independently reconstructed. | All practitioner videos | Reproducible datasets and validation protocol |

None of these candidates may be cited as an approved KRONOS invariant.
