## Underlying Problem

An adverse sequence can be misclassified as system failure, while genuine non-compliance, regime mismatch, or architecture failure can be excused as ordinary uncertainty.

## Proposed Principle

Future review should determine whether KRONOS must distinguish:

- expected uncertainty
- normal adverse sequences
- behavioural non-compliance
- regime mismatch
- architecture failure

These are provisional distinctions. No quantitative boundary or diagnostic rule is proposed.
