# YT-004 — POWER OF STOCKS Option-Selling Course

**Status:** Draft Research  
**Source:** [Options Selling Course — eight-video playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLEK2KlOx2h_HHWOb4FLff4hK-YY03iIIv)  
**Review status:** Playlist and all eight episode summaries reviewed

## Observation

The playlist emphasizes that selling premium is a risk-management and behavioral problem, not free money. It repeatedly favors predefined exits, disciplined capital use, and caution with overnight naked exposure.

## Evidence

- Episode 1 frames selling as defending a price condition until expiry and acknowledges large loss potential.
- Episodes 2–3 discuss probability while warning that a high win rate does not remove disaster risk.
- Episodes 4–6 discuss capital, margin, hedging, moneyness, progression of risk, and psychological pressure.
- Episode 7 argues for stop-and-re-entry over uncontrolled adjustment or "firefighting."
- Episode 8 links moneyness choices to India VIX and personal risk/reward preferences.
- Overnight gaps and the accumulation of many small gains before a large loss recur as cautions.

## Possible Interpretation

The durable research contribution is the need to study loss distribution, tail events, overnight exposure, capital use, and decision discipline together. The playlist's particular rules are not necessary to retain that contribution.

## Open Questions

- Do stop-and-re-entry and adjustment approaches have different stress outcomes after costs?
- How should overnight gap exposure be measured for defined-risk and naked positions?
- Does any probability advantage survive realistic loss severity and friction?
- Which claims generalize beyond index options to MCX options?

## Not Retained

- Claimed 60%–65% seller success probability.
- Claimed fixed percentage of sideways markets.
- India VIX bands for ATM/OTM selection.
- Specific margin buffers, point cushions, stop distances, moneyness progression, or risk/reward ratios.
- Personal profit and account-growth claims.
- Directional or overnight trading instructions.
