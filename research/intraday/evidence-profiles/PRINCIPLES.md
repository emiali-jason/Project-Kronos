## Interpretation Boundary

- `I1` records that independence among supporting sources is not established.
- `E1` records that current proposition support is conceptual, anecdotal, illustrative, or practitioner-based rather than systematic direct validation.
- Ranges such as `M1–M2` describe differences among source records; they are not uncertainty intervals or scores.
- No profile in this register is an accepted principle or architectural conclusion.
