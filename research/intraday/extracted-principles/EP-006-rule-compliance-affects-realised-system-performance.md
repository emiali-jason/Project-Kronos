## Extracted Proposition

The realised performance of a decision process depends partly on whether authorised actions are followed throughout the lifecycle.

## Extraction Boundary

This does not define authorised actions or assume that human behavioural issues map directly to automation.

## Validation Question

Which deviations belong to decision, risk, execution, or position-management responsibility?
