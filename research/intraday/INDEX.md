## Column Semantics

- **Source Status** records the standing of an individual registered source. `PL-*` rows are `COLLECTION_REFERENCE` records rather than sources.
- **Review Status** records source-review maturity. Existing `STRUCTURED` records are understood to have passed the newly explicit `REVIEWED` gate.
- **Architecture Traceability** is a controlled relationship label: `NONE`, `TOPIC_ONLY`, `SUPPORTS_EXISTING_EP`, `ORIGINATES_AC`, `VALIDATION_EVIDENCE`, or `ACCEPTED_REFERENCE`. It is not an approval or implementation claim.
- **Candidate Links** counts distinct existing `AC-*` records linked through the source's existing EP or candidate traceability. It must not be summed to calculate candidate records created because one candidate can link to multiple sources.
