# Capability Candidates

**Status:** Draft Research  
**Architecture Status:** Not proposed or approved

These are candidate subjects for future product discovery. They define neither components nor owners, interfaces, schemas, priorities, or implementation.

| ID | Capability candidate | Research purpose | Evidence required before promotion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CC-001 | Source provenance and claim traceability | Preserve what was observed, by whom, and with what authority | Source-quality and citation review |
| CC-002 | Option-chain snapshot research | Study prices, Greeks, OI, volume, spread, and expiry state together | Reliable historical and live data assessment |
| CC-003 | Contract-lifecycle awareness | Study expiry, exercise, assignment, devolvement, and delivery exposure | Exchange and broker documentation plus operational examples |
| CC-004 | Position and leg exposure review | Study delta, gamma, theta, vega, skew, and concentration | Validated Greek histories and reconciliation |
| CC-005 | Adjustment and roll lifecycle replay | Reconstruct how each action changed the combined position | Complete orders, fills, costs, intent, and timestamps |
| CC-006 | Liquidity and executability assessment | Distinguish theoretical opportunity from tradable opportunity | Bid/ask, depth, slippage, rejection, and exit evidence |
| CC-007 | Volatility and event-context research | Study IV, realized movement, term structure, skew, and events | Multi-regime synchronized datasets |
| CC-008 | Cross-market context study | Test COMEX, MCX, currency, and session relationships | Aligned market histories and lead-lag analysis |
| CC-009 | Stress-event reconstruction | Attribute outcomes during gaps, volatility expansion, or liquidity loss | Event timeline and position lifecycle evidence |
| CC-010 | Futures-intent traceability | Separate hedges, adjustments, and directional futures trades | Contemporaneous intent and linkage records |
| CC-011 | Short-horizon buying review | Study movement speed, contract responsiveness, decay, IV, and friction | Underlying and option execution replay |
| CC-012 | Evidence-based research checklist | Make missing facts and unresolved assumptions visible | Repeated evidence reviews showing stable information needs |

Names in this table are research labels, not approved services, engines, modules, or backlog commitments.
