# Extracted Principles

Extracted principles are source-faithful propositions positioned between research notes and architecture candidates. They are not approved KRONOS principles and do not authorise design or implementation.

Each record lists its supporting sources and links to a research-only evidence profile, convergence assignment, and consensus record. Source counts do not establish validity or evidence independence.
