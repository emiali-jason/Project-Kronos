# Intraday Validation Programmes

The Trading Desk never modifies KRONOS.

It observes, validates and records.

Validation documents contain evidence only.

Engineering decisions belong exclusively to Master Architecture.

## Programmes

| Programme | Purpose | Log |
|---|---|---|
| KR-315 Compression Validation | Record observations where Compression is the Active Blocker | [KR-315 Compression Validation](KR-315-compression-validation.md) |
| KR-321 Global-Local Synchronization | Record cases where global market leads, local market waits, and local market confirms later | [KR-321 Global-Local Synchronization](KR-321-global-local-synchronization.md) |
| KR-330 Promotion Validation | Record timing and evidence during WAIT to WATCH to BUY READY to BUY NOW promotion | [KR-330 Promotion Validation](KR-330-promotion-validation.md) |
| KR-340 Decision Pipeline Metrics | Record daily decision inventory and transition-flow metrics for each review universe | [KR-340 Decision Pipeline Metrics](KR-340-decision-pipeline-metrics.md) |
