## Observation

The source presents a repeatable process intended to discourage breakout chasing and repeated participation in indecisive markets.

## Evidence

- Underlying trend context is considered before the option contract.
- Confirmation tools are presented as a way to distinguish direction from sideways noise.
- Repeated participation in choppy conditions is discouraged.
- Acceptable loss and exit are considered before entry.
- Session-level limits and mental capital are discussed as responses to overtrading.

## Possible Interpretation
