## Extracted Proposition

A favourable outcome does not prove a valid decision, and an adverse outcome does not prove an invalid decision.

## Extraction Boundary

This proposition does not define decision validity, classification logic, or acceptance criteria.

## Validation Question

Can decision validity be assessed using contemporaneous information without outcome leakage?
