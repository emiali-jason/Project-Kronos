# Books

Research notes derived from books belong here. Record edition and page provenance; do not treat an author's claim as an approved principle.

- Structured notes: [`notes/`](notes/)
- Source provenance: [`source-manifests/`](source-manifests/)

Copyrighted book files are not copied into the repository. A source manifest records the inspected filename, hash, bibliographic metadata, extraction quality, and rights status without retaining an absolute filesystem path.
